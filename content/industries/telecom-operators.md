## The Challenge

Mobile network operators face constant pressure to demonstrate network performance, diagnose interference and degradation quickly, and plan capacity expansion across diverse spectrum bands including 4G LTE and 5G NR. Drive testing and network probing have traditionally required dedicated measurement vehicles and specialist teams, creating high per-measurement costs and slow feedback loops. At the same time, the volume of KPI data generated by a national network exceeds what any manual analysis process can handle.

## Why It Matters

- **Revenue impact of poor KPIs:** Dropped calls, slow data throughput, and high handover failure rates drive churn. For a national MNO, a 1% improvement in call setup success rate translates directly to measurable revenue retention.
- **5G rollout complexity:** 5G NR introduces new measurement dimensions — beam tracking, millimetre-wave coverage, network slicing performance — that require new measurement toolchains.
- **Regulatory reporting:** Telecom regulators in Iran and regional export markets require documented network quality KPIs on a defined reporting schedule.
