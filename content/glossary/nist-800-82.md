## Definition

NIST SP 800-82 is the US National Institute of Standards and Technology's Guide to Industrial Control System (ICS) Security. It provides guidance on applying NIST's cybersecurity framework to SCADA, DCS, and PLC environments.

## How It Works

NIST 800-82 recommends a defence-in-depth approach for ICS security, with network segmentation, access control, monitoring, and incident response as key controls. It specifically recommends unidirectional security gateways (data diodes) as a Conduit control for high-consequence zones where software-based firewalls do not provide sufficient assurance.

## Why It Matters for Network Security

NIST 800-82 is widely cited in government procurement specifications and OT security assessments globally, including by Iranian critical-infrastructure operators benchmarking against international standards.
