## OTX-2 | Product Overview

OTX-2 is a 1U rack-mount OTN/SDH MuxPonder designed and manufactured by Partov Ertebat Saba. The device aggregates multiple SDH tributary streams and Gigabit Ethernet services onto OTN transport links, enabling efficient utilisation of optical transport network capacity in carrier and government network infrastructures.

With 2 × OTN SFP uplink ports, 3 × SDH SFP tributary ports, and 3 × 1 Gbps RJ45 Ethernet ports, OTX-2 bridges legacy SDH infrastructure with modern OTN optical transport — providing flexible protocol conversion and traffic aggregation in a single compact 1U chassis.

## Architecture

- **2 × SFP OTN ports** — optical transport network uplink interfaces
- **3 × SFP SDH ports** — SDH tributary input/output
- **3 × RJ45 Ethernet ports** — 1 Gbps Ethernet service interfaces
- **Console port** — out-of-band management and configuration
- **USB dongle port** — licence and configuration key interface

## Key Features

- OTN/SDH MuxPonder — aggregates SDH and Ethernet services onto OTN transport
- 2 × OTN SFP uplink ports for optical transport network connectivity
- 3 × SDH SFP tributary ports for legacy SDH network integration
- 3 × 1 Gbps RJ45 Ethernet ports for IP/Ethernet service aggregation
- Console and USB dongle management interfaces
- 1U rack-mount form factor with standard rack ears
- Designed for carrier-grade optical transport network environments
