Industrial Data Diode G300 | Product OverviewThe Data Diode G300 is designed and manufactured to establish unidirectional data communication between two Ethernet networks. By completely eliminating any return paths, it physically prevents intrusion, unauthorized access, and cyberattacks from external networks to the secure network.The Data Diode G300 is specifically designed for secure one-way transmission of IP surveillance camera streams. Unlike other data diode models that support general data transfer, industrial messaging, or various network services, this model is optimized for stable and secure video streaming in surveillance and industrial monitoring environments. This specialization makes the G300 an ideal solution for security projects, control centers, and infrastructures relying on IP camera systems.In this system, the one-way nature of data flow is ensured through full hardware-based separation of the receive and transmit paths, combined with the creation of a unidirectional link using fiber optics. In addition, the transmit and receive sections are completely isolated even at the power supply level, eliminating any possibility of data leakage through power paths and allowing the device to function as a secure network isolator.The Data Diode G300 is a fully hardware-based, OS-less system with no operating system or general-purpose processor. As a result, there are no attack vectors for hacking, malware execution, or exploit-based attacks. The device is built on next-generation FPGA technology, and by implementing the full Ethernet stack at the hardware level, it enables high and stable data transfer rates.The Data Diode G300 is ready for operation without any initial configuration or activation of management protocols such as SNMP, minimizing the risk of security vulnerabilities caused by misconfiguration.
