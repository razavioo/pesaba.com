Industrial Data Diode G200 | Product OverviewThe Industrial Data Diode G200 is a one-way data transfer device (One-Way Data Diode) designed to provide complete isolation between secure and non-secure networks.By completely eliminating all return paths, this system prevents intrusion, cyberattacks, and data manipulation at a fundamental level. It is an ideal solution for protecting critical infrastructures, control centers, and OT/ICS networks.The G200 is built on a hardware-based, OS-less architecture, ensuring high reliability and fast data transmission without the risks associated with operating systems. This data diode supports a wide range of network protocols and enables the secure transfer of logs, monitoring data, SCADA messages, and telemetry information from internal networks to external networks.This product is specifically designed for energy, oil and gas, transportation industries, control rooms, management centers, and mission-critical systems that require unidirectional security, guaranteeing that no data can ever flow back into the sensitive network.Key Features of the Industrial Data Diode G200Unidirectional communication between two Ethernet networks (One-Way Data
