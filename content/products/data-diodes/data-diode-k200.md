Industrial Data Diode K200 | Product OverviewThe Data Diode K200 is a hardware-based security system designed and developed to establish unidirectional data communication between two Ethernet networks. By completely eliminating all return paths, this device prevents intrusion, unauthorized access, and cyberattacks from external networks to the secure network.In the Data Diode K200, the unidirectional flow of data is ensured through complete hardware-level separation of the transmit and receive sections, along with the creation of a one-way link using fiber optics. In addition, the transmit and receive sections are fully isolated even at the power supply level, physically eliminating any possibility of data leakage.This system is fully hardware-based and OS-less, with no operating system or general-purpose processor. As a result, there are no attack vectors for hacking, malware execution, or exploit-based attacks. The architecture of the Data Diode K200 is built on next-generation FPGA technology, and by implementing the full Ethernet stack at the hardware level, it enables stable high-speed data transfer rates of up to 10 Gbps.The Data Diode K200 is ready for operation without any initial configuration or activation of management protocols, minimizing the risk of security vulnerabilities caused by misconfiguration.
