## EMX-9 | Product Overview

EMX-9 is a high-density 1U rack-mount network switching and filtering platform designed and manufactured by Partov Ertebat Saba. The system provides 8 × RJ45 Gigabit Ethernet ports and 10 × SFP ports alongside a console management port and USB dongle interface, targeting carrier, government, and enterprise network aggregation and deep traffic inspection use cases.

Built on FPGA architecture, EMX-9 delivers deterministic, low-latency packet forwarding without the vulnerability surface of a general-purpose operating system. The panel features the characteristic EMX cyan indicator border that marks the EMX-9 Series devices.

## Architecture

- **8 × RJ45 ports** — Gigabit Ethernet copper access
- **10 × SFP ports** — flexible optical or copper uplinks
- **Console port** — out-of-band management
- **USB dongle port** — licence and configuration key interface
- **FPGA data path** — deterministic switching with no OS in the forwarding plane

## Key Features

- 8 × 1 Gbps RJ45 Ethernet ports
- 10 × SFP uplink/expansion ports
- Console port and USB dongle interface for management and licensing
- FPGA-based architecture — no operating system in data path
- Characteristic EMX-9 Series cyan panel border indicator
- 1U rack-mount with standard rack ears
- Suitable for high-density switching and traffic filtering in carrier and government environments
