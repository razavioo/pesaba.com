## EMX-8 | Product Overview

EMX-8 is a 1U rack-mount network switching and filtering appliance designed and manufactured by Partov Ertebat Saba. The device provides 8 × RJ45 Gigabit Ethernet access ports and 8 × SFP uplink ports, making it suitable for enterprise, government, and carrier edge deployments requiring high port-density in a compact 1U form factor.

The appliance is engineered for reliability in mission-critical environments and integrates seamlessly with existing rack infrastructure. Its combination of copper and fibre ports enables flexible connectivity to both standard Ethernet endpoints and optical network aggregation links.

## Architecture

EMX-8 is built around a dedicated switching ASIC with:

- **8 × RJ45 ports** for 1 Gbps copper Ethernet access connections
- **8 × SFP uplink ports** for 1G or 10G fibre or copper SFP modules
- **1U chassis** with standard rack ears for 19-inch rack mounting

## Key Features

- 8 × 1 Gbps RJ45 Ethernet access ports
- 8 × SFP uplink ports supporting 1G and 10G modules
- Compact 1U form factor with rack-mount hardware included
- Status LEDs for rapid fault identification
- Suitable for enterprise, government, and carrier edge environments
