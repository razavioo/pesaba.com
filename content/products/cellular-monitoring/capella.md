## Capella | Product Overview

Capella is a mobile network performance monitoring solution developed by Partov Ertebat Saba as part of the Venus Pioneer suite. It is designed to measure and analyse the quality of mobile functional services — including voice calls, SMS, USSD, data throughput, and multimedia streaming — and to extract KPI and KQI indicators for comprehensive network evaluation.

Capella enables operators and regulators to assess network performance at any hour of the day and at specific busy locations — routes, roads, terminals, malls, or commercial areas. The system produces a range of analytical reports that reflect the actual quality of experience (QoE) of subscribers using the target services.

All measured data is stored in the Venus Pioneer system and displayed as visual reports or exportable output.

## Key Features

- Extraction and collection of cell power and location data from serving and neighbour cells
- Full support for 2G, GPRS, UMTS, LTE, and TD-LTE radio technologies
- KPI extraction: CSSR, TCH ASR, CDR, Cell Unavailability, and more
- KQI extraction: Throughput (upload/download), Ping, Jitter, and application-level indicators (streaming, email, multimedia)
- Periodic and historical reporting for all service quality parameters
- Real-time alarm monitoring for network nodes and sensors
- Geographic map view — status display per city, province, or defined area

## Integration

Capella integrates with Venus Pioneer for data aggregation, trend analysis, and unified dashboard reporting across the full network. It can run alongside Auriga and Venus Netsens as part of an end-to-end measurement chain.
