## Venus Pioneer | Product Overview

Venus Pioneer is the central data aggregation, analysis, and reporting application of the Venus suite. It consolidates measurements from Auriga, Capella, Venus Netsens, and Atlas sensors — whether deployed in moving vehicles or fixed locations — into a unified platform. Operators gain continuous, automatic visibility into service quality and subscriber experience without requiring expert personnel to be present at each measurement site.

In many cases, real-time analytics delivered by Pioneer eliminate the need for hours of manual drive testing. When the system identifies the location and root cause of a network issue, further measurement effort becomes unnecessary — reducing operational cost and response time.

## Key Features

- Aggregation of data from all Venus suite sensors (Auriga, Capella, Venus Netsens, Atlas)
- Accurate evaluation of test routes, site locations, and network performance across fixed and mobile sensor deployments
- Continuous and automatic service quality monitoring without expert on-site presence
- Real-time analytics for rapid problem identification and root-cause localisation
- Drive-test execution without driver interaction
- Frequency coverage mapping across all supported radio technologies (2G, 3G, 4G, TD-LTE)
- Continuous remote monitoring and control of QoE and QoS
- Automatic detection and mapping of no-coverage areas
- Video streaming performance analysis
- Geographic map view — status and indicator display per city, province, or custom region
- Offline data processing and geographic data export
- Compatible with vehicle-mounted or fixed-chassis sensor installations
- Signal strength and quality visualisation across test paths and scenarios

## Integration

Venus Pioneer serves as the backend for the entire Venus measurement ecosystem. Capella, Auriga, and Venus Netsens all forward their measurement data to Pioneer, which stores, correlates, and presents the data as visual reports, exportable datasets, and operational dashboards.
