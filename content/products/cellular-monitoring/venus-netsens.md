## Venus Netsens | Product Overview

Venus Netsens is a drive-test and network-probe application used by mobile operators to collect, display, and log cellular network measurement data. By analysing drive-test results, operators can identify and resolve network problems, obtain accurate information on network status and service quality, and improve subscriber satisfaction.

Netsens is an application installable on a smartphone. It displays data collected by Atlas — a background measurement service running in the smartphone core — and organises measurements into three general parameter categories:

- Current channel parameters
- Radio parameters
- Serving and neighbour cell parameters

## Key Features

### Drive Testing and Coverage Measurement

- Full execution of drive tests as the primary task
- Indoor and outdoor network coverage measurement
- Signal strength and quality display across different test-path scenarios
- Automatic identification of no-coverage areas

### KPI and Signal Logging

- Power and signal quality parameters
- Spatial (location) parameters
- Frequency parameters
- All SIB parameters related to cell selection and cell re-selection procedures
- Channel allocation parameters
- Control-plane message logging (RRC/NAS layer) with full content

### Service Testing

- End-to-end testing of voice calls, data services, and SMS for troubleshooting and acceptance
- Network service testing and acceptance
- Recording and interpretation of signalling messages during test calls

### Automation

- Periodic and pre-designed test scripts with scheduling
- Continuous, unattended measurement without driver interaction

## Integration

Venus Netsens feeds measurement data to Venus Pioneer for aggregated reporting, historical analysis, and geographic visualisation across the full operator network.
