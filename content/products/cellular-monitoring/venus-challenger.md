## Venus Challenger | Product Overview

Venus Challenger is a complete, real-time mobile network analysis and optimisation platform for 2G, 3G, and 4G networks. It is used by mobile operators and regulators to understand network behaviour, prevent degradation in service quality, and improve subscriber experience.

The system connects directly to measurement sensors through the database, receiving all necessary measured information without manual data transfer. Beyond measuring QoE, Venus Challenger provides actionable solutions to improve it — making it both a diagnostic and an optimisation tool.

Because QoE measurement is inherently complex, Venus Challenger designs tests that closely replicate actual human behaviour. This approach captures the parameters that matter most to subscribers and gives operators accurate, subscriber-perspective insight into their network. With this intelligence, operators can identify the right moment to expand network capacity and project capacity requirements across defined planning horizons.

## Key Features

- Script design and execution for accurate KPI calculation
- Remote management and configuration of measurement sensors
- Operational intelligence dashboards for network status and subscriber experience
- Continuous, real-time display and measurement of QoE and QoS
- Actionable solutions to raise QoS and QoE levels
- Analytics dashboard for in-depth troubleshooting of voice and data performance
- Execution of timed, human-behaviour-modelling test scripts
- Capacity planning and network expansion forecasting
- Alarm monitoring for network nodes and service thresholds

## Supported Technologies

2G, GPRS, 3G (UMTS), 4G LTE, TD-LTE

## Integration

Venus Challenger integrates with Venus Pioneer and the wider Venus measurement suite, consolidating sensor data into unified reports and enabling correlation across geographic regions and time periods.
