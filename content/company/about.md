## Who We Are

Partov Ertebat Saba (Pesaba) develops network-security hardware and telecommunications equipment in Iran. Its engineering work addresses data transfer, encryption, network visibility, and communications requirements in industrial, governmental, and operator environments. Corporate history and legal status should be verified against current records when required for procurement.

Our work spans electronics, FPGA and embedded design, network protocols, test, and product support. Since its founding, Pesaba has built a portfolio focused on hardware encryption, one-way data transfer, network analysis and monitoring, and telecom transmission.

## What We Do

We design and build:

- **Hardware encryption devices** — OS-less, FPGA-based AES-256 platforms for industrial and operator network links.
- **Data diodes** — hardware one-way gateways intended for OT/ICS and SCADA network boundaries.
- **Network analysers and probes** — full-packet capture and 2G/3G/4G performance monitoring solutions.
- **Telecom transmission equipment** — Ethernet-over-E1 and STM-1 modems for connecting remote sites.
- **Bio-monitoring systems** — continuous biological water-toxicity monitoring and alerting.

## Why Pesaba

Pesaba sources components and selects manufacturing, test, and measurement processes for each product and order. Performance, component origin, warranty, support scope, and commercial terms should be assessed for the quoted model and configuration against the purchaser's requirements.
