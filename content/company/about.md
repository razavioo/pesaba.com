## Who We Are

Partov Ertebat Saba (Pesaba) is a knowledge-based company founded in 2008 (1387 SH) in Tehran, specialising in the design and manufacture of network security hardware and telecommunications equipment. Our mission is to deliver high-specification, domestically engineered solutions to the most demanding network security challenges in industrial, governmental, and operator environments.

Our team is composed of PhD and MSc graduates from Iran's leading universities, working to localise critical network security technology and to deliver the best technical outcome to our customers in the shortest possible time. With more than a decade of work in communications, network security, and information technology, Pesaba has built a portfolio focused on hardware encryption, one-way data transfer, network analysis and monitoring, and telecom transmission.

## What We Do

We design and build:

- **Hardware encryption devices** — OS-less, FPGA-based AES-256 platforms for protecting industrial and operator traffic.
- **Data diodes** — physical one-way gateways for OT/ICS and SCADA isolation.
- **Network analysers and probes** — full-packet capture and 2G/3G/4G performance monitoring solutions.
- **Telecom transmission equipment** — Ethernet-over-E1 and STM-1 modems for connecting remote sites.
- **Bio-monitoring systems** — continuous water-toxicity detection for critical water infrastructure.

## Why Pesaba

Direct sourcing of production components and the use of modern manufacturing, production and quality-measurement technologies allow us to deliver products that compete with the world's leading manufacturers — at prices competitive with East Asian suppliers. Continuous training and after-sales support are core to how we work, and customer demand and satisfaction are our central focus.

Security for us is not just a product line — it is a commitment. We pair domestic engineering with the latest hardware and software technologies to deliver genuine protection of critical infrastructure against cyber threats. Rapid support, responsible communication, and continuous innovation in the design and localisation of security solutions are what make Pesaba a trusted partner.

## Knowledge-Base Mission

We believe that by raising technical understanding and sharing up-to-date information, we can support the growth of the information-technology field for companies, organisations, and individuals who care about it. That is why we publish technical articles on encryption, data diodes, and OT/ICS security alongside our products.
